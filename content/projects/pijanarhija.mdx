Pijanarhija is a digital companion for a local drinking card game, built to bring a traditional social experience into the modern era. As the sole developer, I transformed the game's concept into a fast, responsive static site. The focus was on accessibility and engagement, using smooth scrolling and interactive elements to keep players immersed in the game without unnecessary distractions.
