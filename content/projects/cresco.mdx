Cresco is an AI-powered fitness tracking application designed to push the boundaries of modern web design. This project served as a primary testing ground for advanced motion design and smooth scrolling techniques. By combining Next.js with powerful animation libraries, I created a seamless user experience that visualizes the future of exercise coaching. The focus was on high-fidelity interactions and a clean, dark-themed aesthetic.
