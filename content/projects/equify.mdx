Equfy is a stock market web app where users can explore real-time market data in a simple and usable way. You can search for stocks, view detailed information for each one, and keep track of what matters through a personal watchlist. The app also includes authentication, so users can sign up, log in, and have their own dashboard experience. The focus was on making data feel accessible without overcomplicating the interface.
