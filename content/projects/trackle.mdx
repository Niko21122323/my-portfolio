Trackle is a comprehensive subscription management web app that allows users to regain control over their recurring expenses. It features a full authentication system where users can securely sign up and manage their data. You can add, remove, and edit subscriptions while filtering by status—active, canceled, or expired. The dashboard provides a clear overview of subscription counts and spending, helping users track where their money goes in real-time.
